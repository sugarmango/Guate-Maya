Lake Atitlan


Lake Atitlan also known as "between waters" is located in Solola, in southwest Guatemala.Lake Atitlan is one of the most beautiful lakes in the world ad it's magnificent landscape features three volcanoes on its southern flank. Lake Atitlan is surrounded by 11 Mayan villages that are reached only by boats or roads in the mountains as there is no road that goes around the lake. Among the 11 villages, there are 5 popular tourist destinations, which are Panajachel, San Pedro, San Marcos, Santa Cruz, and Jaibalito. There are several things to do during your stay in Atitlan such as hiking, going around the villages by boat, shopping local handicraft, yoga, paraglading, paddle boarding, and so much more.

We currently offer three packages with prices for two people with the option to customize the lenght of your stay and guests number once you are ready to book. Our services include a tour guide with the option to opt out and explore on your own. Our hikes include Atitlan Volcano, Toliman Volcano, San Pedro Volcano, and the Indian's Nose.
