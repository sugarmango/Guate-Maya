Monterrico Beach


Monterrico beach is located in Santa Rosa, Guatemala on the Pacific Coast.You can expect to enjoy gorgeous sunsets and views at our private beach section or even at the pool by the beach. You may also find some baby sea turtles in Monterrico's black volcanic beach, depending on the time of the year you visit.

We currently offer three packages with prices for two people with the option to customize the lenght of your stay and guests number once you are ready to book. Please contact us for more information. Come find out by yourself what Monterrico has to offer you and we guarantee that you will not be dissapointed.
